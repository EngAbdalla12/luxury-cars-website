ACQUISITION PROCESS

Client Information Handbooks

Select any guide below to understand the protocols and standards of acquiring our vehicles.

I

Buying Guide

Our buying process is structured to guarantee absolute vehicle authenticity. Each vehicle is provided with certified paint measurements, mechanical diagnostic logs, and historical title verification.

Clients can choose between immediate showroom pickup, custom factory specification ordering, or purchasing from our pre-detailed inventory catalog.

II

Financing Guide

We work with leading international banking institutions to arrange tailored leases, competitive balloon financing, and assets-backed credit facilities.

Our specialists can structures agreements in alignment with corporate tax entities or private trust funds, providing maximum financial efficiency.

III

Reservation Guide

To reserve a vehicle from our current inventory, clients must submit an online reservation proposal. This holds the vehicle for 48 hours.

A deposit of 5% is required once our concierge establishes contact. This secures the vehicle for detailing, inspections, and final document drafting.

IV

Delivery & Logistics

We operate our own fleet of custom enclosed transporters. Weather-controlled and highly secure, our carriers protect your automobile from dirt, road stones, and rain during transit.

We offer full maritime and air freight logistics for international clients, completing customs clearance procedures on your behalf.

COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Review immediate answers to our patrons' most frequent showroom inquiries.

Can I customize a vehicle's specifications before delivery?

Yes. Luxury Cars operates a dedicated bespoke detailing and customization studio. We can modify paint protection films, apply high-end ceramic coatings, upgrade exhaust systems, or install custom interior leather finishes prior to handover.

What certification does a certified pre-owned luxury vehicle receive?

Every pre-owned vehicle undergoes an intensive 150-point mechanical, structural, and cosmetic inspection by our factory-trained technicians. We check engine compression, paint thickness, suspension bushings, and electronics, and perform full road testing. Vehicles are delivered with a signed certificate of quality.

Do you accept trades of standard or classic vehicles?

We accept trade-ins of high-end luxury vehicles, modern exotics, and select classic sports cars. Our valuation experts conduct on-site inspections or review digital portfolios to provide highly competitive valuations aligned with current global auctions.

How does international delivery work for non-US residents?

We coordinate secure sea or air shipping. Our logistics department drafts bills of lading, prepares export customs forms, and handles international transport insurance. The vehicle is delivered to your designated port or coordinates with local flatbeds to drop at your garage door.
