OUR BUSINESS STORY

A Quarter Century of Automotive Passion

Luxury Cars was founded by a team of motorsport enthusiasts and luxury curators with a simple, singular goal: to create a sanctuary where buying a high-end automobile is as thrilling and refined as driving one.

We started as a boutique showroom in Midtown Manhattan with only six vehicles. Today, we are one of the nation's premier automotive dealerships, connecting collectors and performance drivers with the finest exotics, luxury SUVs, and sports coupes from the world's most elite brands.

Over the past 25 years, our brand has become synonymous with trust, premium detailing, and bespoke customer service. We continue to evolve, matching classic mechanical engineering with modern hybrid and electric innovation.

🎯

Our Mission

OUR PILLARS

The Values That Drive Us

Every decision we make, from vehicle selection to client negotiation, is guided by our core tenets.

Integrity First

Transparency is our standard. Every vehicle in our showroom is delivered with fully authenticated mechanical histories and complete certification documentation.

Bespoke Excellence

No two clients are identical. We customize every detail of the transaction, from customized financing to tailored, white-glove enclosed transport.

Passion for Performance

We are drivers first. We choose vehicles that possess character, exceptional engineering, and distinct automotive styling.

Sustainability Focus

We actively offset the carbon footprint of our delivery fleet and advocate for the advancement of high-performance electric supercars.
